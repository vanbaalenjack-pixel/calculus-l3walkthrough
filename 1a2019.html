Learning summary

Review the method, not only the answer

This walkthrough focuses on chain rule differentiation of a square root. Afterwards, try the same method again without hints and explain the reasoning that connects each step.

Common mistake to avoid

Do not stop after differentiating the outside function; include the derivative of the inside function as a factor.
