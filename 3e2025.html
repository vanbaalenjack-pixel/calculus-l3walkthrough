Learning summary

Review the method, not only the answer

This walkthrough focuses on maximising the area of a rectangle inside a curve. Afterwards, try the same method again without hints and explain the reasoning that connects each step.

Common mistake to avoid

Finding a stationary value is only part of an optimisation argument; justify that it is the required maximum and respect the domain.
