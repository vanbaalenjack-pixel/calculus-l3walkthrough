This site provides interactive walkthroughs of the NCEA Level 3 Calculus exam. Each question is broken into small steps to help you understand the reasoning behind the solution, not just the final answer.

Work through each step, read the feedback, and move to the next stage once you understand the idea. The goal is to help you build the thinking required for Achieved, Merit, and Excellence answers.
